# Code of conduct

## Our standard

FrontierIQ is a professional collaboration space. We expect contributors to:

- communicate respectfully and assume good intent
- give feedback that is specific, constructive, and actionable
- disagree with ideas without attacking people
- keep security, privacy, and tenant impact in mind when discussing changes

## Unacceptable behavior

The following behavior is not acceptable in project spaces, including issues,
pull requests, reviews, and discussions:

- harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- discriminatory or demeaning language
- bad-faith disruption, trolling, or deliberate escalation
- sharing secrets, personal data, or tenant-specific information without a clear
  operational need

## Reporting concerns

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer
through a private GitHub discussion or the repository security reporting channel
when the concern is security-related.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, lock, or moderate content that violates this code
of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent
loss of participation privileges.
